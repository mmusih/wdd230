White Space and Clean Design

Instapaper

www.instapaper.com

This design has made use of negative to guide the user’s attention to who they are and what they do. The detail that is there is minimal and aimed at conversion, the user sees what they are all about and immediately below there is the create account button. This shows that negative space in never wasted space.

Visual Hierarchy

Fullstory

www.fullstory.com

The design makes use of visual hierarchy in the sense that when one first views the page, one is immediately drawn to the bold letters, which tell the user what the website is all about, then one can follow the natural order of reading for more detail. They use contrast of colors to bring the attention of the user to the learn more button.

Hick's Law

Amazon

www.amazon.com

To avoid user frustration with limitless choices they will have to make when browsing products, amazon has categorized their products and enabled filters to ensure quick decision making in the buying process. Even though there is a huge selection the user is not confused due to the presentation and proper use of Hick’s Law.
