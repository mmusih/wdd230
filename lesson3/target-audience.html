Description

Members will be established companies as well as startups with the desire to make their brand well known.

Personas

Mark:

My company is not getting new customers and for while there has not been an increase in sales and the company is not growing. I wish there was a way to get my brand noticeable and in front of a broad audience.

Jane:

I want to start a new company and am worried about time it would take to get established. I wish there was a way to get my brand our there in the shortest time possible.

Scenarios

  • A company wishes to produce a new product and at the same time wishes that customers could know about it.
  • An individual wishes to start a company and wants to have a wider audience from the get go.
  • A company wants their contacts to be listed in a categorized business directory.
  • Members wishes to educate his/her employees on latest business trends through seminars provided by Bulls Chamber.
  • Company or individual wishes to have contacts of potential suppliers through hosted networking events.
